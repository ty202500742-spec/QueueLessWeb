About us

We exist to end the waiting game.

Queueless is a digital queue management system created for WMSU — eliminating long lines so students can spend time on what actually matters.

Long queues at university offices wastes hours of student's time every day. QUEUELESS changes that by moving the waiting experience online — you join a queue from your phone, track your position live, and only show up when it's truly your turn.

Designed for students Built around the real daily frustrations of WMSU students at the registrar, cashier, and admin offices.
Real-time position tracking Always know exactly where you are in the queue with live updates and notifications.
No app download needed Works directly in your browser — no installation, no hassle.
